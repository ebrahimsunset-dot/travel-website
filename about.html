About Me

Hello Everyone

My name is Hossain MD Ebrahim, and I’m currently studying at Tokyo International University in the Department of International Relations.

Why I Love Travel

Travel is my passion. Exploring new places gives me a sense of refreshment that nothing else quite matches. Whether it’s a quiet town or a bustling city, I love discovering new streets, new faces, and new stories. Photography goes hand in hand with this — capturing a single frame lets me hold onto a moment long after I’ve left it behind.

Life in Japan

Since coming to Japan, I’ve fallen in love with camping. There’s something about getting away from the city, setting up a tent under the open sky, and waking up to fresh mountain air that keeps pulling me back. It’s become one of my favorite ways to unwind and reconnect with nature while living abroad.

What Drives Me

Between my studies in international relations and my love for travel and photography, I’m always looking to understand people and places more deeply — one trip, one photo, one campsite at a time.
